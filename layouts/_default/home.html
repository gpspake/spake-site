Selling a Home

If you are considering selling your Memphis home, Contact me today for a free, no obligation, market analysis and I will email you the results. However, like some popular real estate portals, the "numbers" do not necessarily reflect your home’s features and amenities, and other factors like location and micro view of your neighborhood. Selling a home can be a stressful event. Decisions are best made when you have all the facts at hand. I would be happy to come to your home and provide market information as well as make suggestions about marketing your home: pricing strategy, staging, repairs, showings, etc.
